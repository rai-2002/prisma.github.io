Neeve Cauchi

Illustrator
Animator

Artist Statement

Neeve is a mixed media artist with a strong passion for illustration, graphic novels, and traditional animation. His art tends to explore personal and mature themes through a gritty and sketchy art style. He hopes to one day illustrate his own novel and possibly lecture.

Since their conception button badges have allowed individuals to display personal aspects of themselves for anyone to see. Neeve’s dissertation “Self-expression through Button badges” puts his identity on full display through a self archive of hand made badges, coming together to create an outfit which represents his human experience.
