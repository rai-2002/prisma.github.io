Artist Statement

Laura Zammit is a mixed-media artist who experiments with unusual materials at any chance she gets. She has many interests within the art sector from set making to fashion design, and hopes to use these varied skills within the animation industry taking charge of her future projects.

By using scavenged materials and thorough experimentation, the project aims to explore the fine line between art and waste.
