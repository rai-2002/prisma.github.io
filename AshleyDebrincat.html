Ashley Debrincat

3D Artist
Animator

Artist Statement

Ashley's artistic journey began in the classic realm of painting and sculpture, but during her university years, she found herself unexpectedly charmed by 3D animation, particularly in 3D modeling and character designâ€”a field she now aspires to excel in professionally.

In her dissertation, Ashley probes the potential of artificial intelligence to mimic or augment human artistic flair, examining AI's cultural and economic ramifications within the arts industry. Her research seeks to discern the emotive resonance, creative expression, and complexity distinguishing AI-generated art from its human craftsmanship. As she delves into the boundaries of what AI can achieve, her work adds a vital perspective to the discourse around the significance of traditional artisanship in an increasingly digital age. This scholarly inquiry led her to collaborate closely with Dall-E, which sparked her imagination to produce 3D renderings of fantastical creatures.
